# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

## Overview

ShipPilot for WooCommerce processes shipping-related information only to provide shipment creation, label generation, and shipment tracking services.

## Information Processed

The plugin may process:

- WooCommerce order information
- Recipient name
- Shipping address
- Phone number
- Email address
- Shipment identifiers
- Tracking numbers

## API Credentials

API credentials entered by the merchant are stored securely within the WordPress installation and are used exclusively for communication with the configured shipping provider.

## Third-Party Services

Depending on configuration, shipment information may be transmitted to external shipping provider APIs.

## Data Storage

ShipPilot does not collect analytics or transmit merchant data to the developer.

## User Rights

Users retain all rights provided under applicable privacy regulations, including GDPR and local privacy laws.
